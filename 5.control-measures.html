Control measures

Reducing similar types of fraud in the future
Warning Signs
  • Similar email formats/incorrect details
  • Past flights claimed retroactively on new accounts
  • Narrow window between claim and redemptions
  • One-way tickets to destinations
  • Immediate rewards signaling quick gain intention
  • Possible account takeover for reported cases
Recommended Checks
  • New FPP account details (Phone, email, country..etc)
  • Transaction history since account creation
  • Compare guest names on tickets vs. account
  • Locations vs booked flights origins/destination
  • Destination flights redeemed concentration
  • Retroclaimed flights volumes history & routes patterns
  • Password or account details change for reported cases
Suspected Fraud Actions
  • Suspension/hold for accounts matching patterns
  • Identify and hold any pending/future dated transactions
  • Correlate similar fingerprint(account details, emails, IPs..) accross other accounts
  • Track significant redemptions/claims for unusual transactions
General measures to reduce fraud
  • Enable member self-service account freeze with one-click activation via app/email link, automatically triggered when suspicious activity alerts are sent
  • Add transaction control options (similar to credit cards), like allow/stop reward shop redemptions, set a redemption limit, or choose which transactions to go through which requires passenger authentication
  • Introduce passkey/biometric authentication for account access, providing phishing-resistant authentication that eliminates vulnerabilities
  • Passkey authentication for redemptions, retroclaims, profile updates, logins from new devices, or reward shop transactions
Supporting customer during fraud
  • Coordinate with partners/stakeholders to stop any future transactions/orders where possible
  • While members are responsible for account security, confirmed account takeover fraud is not the member's financial liability. Restore miles when unauthorized activity is verified
  • If suspended due to confirmed account takeover, re-authenticate member before unlocking & force stricter security measures
Reward shop redemptions
  • Accounts created less than 7 days and redeeming past flights at a quick rate get a hold trigger for 48-hour on all reward redemptions
  • Any redemption value exceeding 40% of the account's total mile balance requires mandatory authentication verification
  • Introduce passkey/biometric authentication for account access, providing phishing-resistant authentication that eliminates vulnerabilities
  • Passkey authentication for redemptions, retroclaims, profile updates, logins from new devices, or reward shop transactions
